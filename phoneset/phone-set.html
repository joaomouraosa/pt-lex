Phone Set for European Portuguese

This phoneset was adapted from: https://www.phon.ucl.ac.uk/home/sampa/portug.htm

Motivation for changes:

  • use lower-case alphabetical characters only.
  • remove vowel and diphtongs unique phones. Instead, we treat them separately
  • add missing velar liquid

Format:

  • syllable marker: -
  • syllable primary stress: 1
  • syllable secondary stress: 2
  • Example: caro: k a 1 - r u
